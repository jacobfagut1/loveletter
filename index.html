It's because every time I'm with you, that's the only time I feel safe. You calm my mind, stop me from overthinking, and it makes me feel relaxed and sleepy.

I want you to feel excited every birthday you have, and I never want you to feel like you don't need or have to celebrate it. It hurts me so much knowing that you feel that way.

I'm also really proud of you, loveyyy. I see how hard you try in everything you do and how strong you are even when things are difficult. You inspire me to become a better person every day, and I'm grateful that I get to see you grow and achieve the things you work hard for.

Loveyyy, you changed my life in so many ways that you might not even realize. Before you, I didn't know how to express my feelings properly, and I kept many things to myself. But because of you, I slowly learned how to open up, communicate better, and become a better person. You helped me grow into someone I'm proud to be today.

I also appreciate the small things you do for me that you might think are simple but mean a lot to me. The way you check on me, remind me to eat, ask about my day, and care about my feelings. Those little things make me feel loved and remind me how lucky I am to have you.

I promise that I will keep trying to be a better boyfriend for you. I know I'm not perfect, and I still make mistakes, but I will always keep learning and improving for us. I want to become someone you can always rely on, someone who will always support you and stay beside you no matter what happens.

I know we still have a lot to go through together, and I'm excited for all the things we will experience in the future. No matter what challenges come our way, I want you to know that I will always choose you. I want to keep growing with you, supporting you, and building more memories with you in the years to come.

Thank you for loving me, for being patient with me, and for staying with me through everything.

Happy 23rd birthday, loveyyy. I love you so much, and I'm thankful every day that I get to call you mine.

No matter how many years pass, I will always be grateful that our paths crossed and that we chose each other. I hope we continue to grow, laugh, and build a beautiful life together. I love you always, loveyyy.

I hope you always remember how loved you are, not just today on your birthday, but every single day, loveyyy.

Four years with you still doesn't feel like enough. I can't wait to spend many more birthdays and anniversaries with you, loveyyy.

With all my love,
Forever yours 💕
